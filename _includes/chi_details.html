Where to Stay

If you are staying the night or longer, then here are a few options with regards to accommodation...

1. Stay near Shillingstone

Shillingstone is the epitome of English countryside and if you want to stay clear of the hustle and bustle then there are plenty of Airbnbs local to Shillingstone.


Blandford Forum is also 10 minute journey away and boasts hotels likeThe Crown.

The Shillingstone House website also has some ideas


The chosen venue allows camping and campervans should you wish to stay the overnight at the venue. You will not need to vacate the premises Sunday and you will have access to the toilet facilities as these don't get taken away till Monday.If you wish to do this please let us know in the RSVP so we can gauge numbers.


2. Stay in Poole / Bournemouth

If you are staying longer than just one night you may want to be in accessible distance to the beach and towns of Poole and Bournemouth. Staying in Poole would mean you are closer to the Church. Check out Airbnbs close to our home and the Church Also see Booking.com hotels nearby

Be mindful that East Dorset is a busy summer holiday destination and with our wedding being in school holidays the area will be busier and more expensive than usual. However at the time of writing we found plenty of smaller places available. If you need any help don't hesitate to ask.
